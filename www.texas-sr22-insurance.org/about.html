We welcome you to browse our web site and enquire further if you have an interest in any of the products offered or if you just have related questions.

What Is an Independent Agency

An Independent Agency is not bound or contracted to any single Insuring Company or Entity,
The advantage of Independence is that it allows a broader range of products from many different Insurers small and large. This also allows us to place coverage for almost any risk within reason, we rarely have to turn down a client needing insurance. We typically can provide Placement and Servicing through 12 to 18 different programs offered by various carriers at any given time. A disadvantage is that they will not offer some carriers who prefer to operate only though exclusive captured agents.
