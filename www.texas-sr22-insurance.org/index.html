Where do I get one ?

"Only Your Insurer" can Issue and sign your insurance certificate on your behalf. Your Insurer may file the SR22 in Texas for you or give it to you to file yourself but only the company may Issue the certificate. It is unlawful for any other entity, or 3'rd party to issue a Certificate on your behalf without your companies consent and can void your policy under fraud and forgery statutes.

What is an SR22 ?

Answer: It's just ";Auto, or Car Insurance with an SR22 certificate", or other coverage such as an Regular Operators or a Non Owners insurance Policy, dui insurance, Where the Insured has Requested Financial Responsibility filing for the state from the Insurance company. The Sr 22 insurance form is an endorsement to your auto liability insurance policy. This is usually the result of a Traffic Ticket for Driving Without the minimum financial responsibility (aka Auto liability Insurance), a DUI in Texas or other citations such as a charge for Possession of Controlled Substances, accidents or other traffic related incidents that may not have been covered by your insurance at the time.
30/60/25 or 85 total is the minimum auto liability insurance required limits indicating verifiable compliance for the state on behalf of the named insured

SR22 Texas Filing Term

The most common and customary sr 22 insurance filing term is for 2 to 3 years from the date of order. Requirements vary depending on your case so you should check with your jurisdiction. Certain offenses may have court ordered terms longer than 3 years.

Responsibilities during Term

Make sure your insurer is aware of your requirement
Keep your policy active to avoid possible suspension. make premium payments on time and be sure your Company is aware of your requirements
Keep your mailing address current. It is required by law anyway and is also the address where the Department of Public Safety sends mail regarding licensing status and updates
Don't drive without insurance coverage. If you have a lapse, cease driving and replace your insurance coverage and SR22 as soon as possible

Penalty for Failure to Maintain DUI, SR22 insurance, TX

You need to maintain your sr22 filing until your required filing period has expired. Your insurer has been required by law to notify the state by cancellation of your SR 22 when your attached auto insurance policy lapses, If not replaced with another insurance certificate, then your Drivers License along with vehicle tags and registrations can again be suspended.

I No longer Need an SR 22 Endorsement

Your Auto Insurance coverage continues in force unless you request otherwise. If you wish you may request the SR22 Endorsement be removed when no longer needed. Remember, proof of Financial Responsibility is required for all drivers when on public roads in Texas. Our Underwriters usually already know of your driving history change, at the next renewal date your insurance premium rate is adjusted to reflect your most recent driving experience.
